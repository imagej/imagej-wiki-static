Widget:Flash

This widget allows you to add SWF content to your wiki page:

  1. Upload the SWF file.
  2. Embed it in a page using 
    {{#widget:flash|swf=<raw-path-to-file>}}

Example

{{#widget:flash|width=993|height=776|swf=3/3e/User_guide_TrakEM.swf}}
