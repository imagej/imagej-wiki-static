Template:PersonList

Usage

Specify a list of people as per the person template. If the list exceeds five people, only the first five are shown, with an ellipsis to expand the complete list.

Examples

{{PersonList|Rasband|Rueden|Dietzc|marktsuchida|Jane Doe|Jack Frost}}

Wayne Rasband, Curtis Rueden, Christian Dietz, Mark Tsuchida, Jane DoeJack Frost 

{{PersonList|Pietzsch|Saalfeld|StephanP}}

Tobias Pietzsch, Stephan Saalfeld, Stephan Preibisch
